Want More?

If you would like to start your own garden you will need a container, soil, water and a few seeds. If you do not have seeds you can also try to grow plant from another plant but I can explain that another time. First you will put your soil in the container. Then you will bury the seed about an inch from the top in the soil. Next you will water the soil. Pour the water in lightly til water seeps through . Don't do it too fast or let it overflow because the seeds may fall out, I know from experience. Then find a place with good sunlight to put your container. Finally remember to water the container every few days. One way to water it is by sticking your finger in the soil, about an inch or two deep , to see if the soil is wet or dry. If it is dry water it if it is wet, wait til try to water it so you won't get root rot.
